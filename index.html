Ball Collector Game

Instructions for this Game!

  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game. "NUMBER TO GET"
  2. There are FOUR Balls Below
  3. By Clicking on a Ball, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  4. You win the game by mathcing your total score to the random number.
  5. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  6. The value of the Ball is hidden from the player until clicked on.
  7. Each time the game starts, the values of the Ball will also change.
  8. Good Luck!!!
